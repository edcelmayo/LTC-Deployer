About Us

Linksphere Technologies Corp.

    Founded in the Philippines in 2012, we began as a dedicated provider of semiconductor test spare parts and board repair services. This foundational expertise quickly established us as a trusted partner for both local and international customers.

    Recognizing the need for a more integrated approach, we have strategically evolved our business. By partnering with Excigence Asia Inc https://www.excigence.com., we have expanded our service portfolio to include a full spectrum of Test Development and engineering capabilities.

    Our journey from specialized supplier to full-service provider reflects our unwavering commitment to meeting the evolving needs of our clients. We are dedicated to providing the expertise and support required to thrive in the demanding world of semiconductor test.

Mission and Vision

MISSION
To be the trusted partner for semiconductor companies by delivering comprehensive, high-quality aftermarket services that maximize the reliability, performance, and cost-effectiveness of every product.


VISION
To be recognized as the global leader in the Aftermarket and Test Development services for the semiconductor test industry.
